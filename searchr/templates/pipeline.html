Analysis Pipeline


Our analysis pipeline showcases its ability to query >800,000 DNA methylation sites that have passed stringent quality control and visualises the resulting data in an intuitive way. We have utilised the Illumina Epic array to provide comprehensive coverage of DNA methylation within the human brain. The Illumina Epic array enables quantitative investigation of selected methylation sites across the human genome. Methylation data provides an opportunity to investigate hyper- and hypomethylation which not only impact gene expression but have further implications in biological processes and diseases. Our workflow begins with collating prefrontal cortex samples from the human brain. In total, 136 samples were collated and run on the Epic array. During quality control and normalisation 45 samples were removed as a results of failed samples, duplicate samples, cerebellum samples and child samples.
